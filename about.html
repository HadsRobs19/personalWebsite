Hadassah Roberts is currently a 2nd year student at the University of Central Florida in Orlando, Florida. She is studying to earn her BFA in Emerging Media with a focus on Character Animation. She focusing on using the physical medium of ink and has an overarching concept of surrealistic concepts and transcendentalistic themes in her work. Hadassah has had experience in the art industry through her involvement in the 2021 Scholastics Art and Writing Awards with one silver key and an honorable mention. She was also recently accepted into a UCF affiliated art gallery taking place in late fall 2022.
